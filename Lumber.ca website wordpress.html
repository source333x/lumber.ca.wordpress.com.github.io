Our Story

Built from the Ground Up, Board by Board

LUMBER.CA was founded in 1989 in Vancouver, BC by a family of foresters who believed the construction industry deserved a lumber partner that combined old-world craftsmanship with modern logistics. What started as a small sawmill operation grew into Canada's most trusted wholesale lumber network.

Today we operate 12 distribution centres from Victoria to Halifax, serving over 4,200 contractor accounts with same-week delivery, competitive wholesale pricing, and a product range that covers every stage of the build.

Our promise hasn't changed: deliver the right wood, to the right site, at the right time — every time.
